# Claims Denial Prediction - ML Model
LIVE DEMO 
AI-powered prediction system for insurance claims denial risk

## Business Problem

10-15% of insurance claims get denied, impacting cash flow. Predicting denial risk enables pre-submission correction.

## What It Does

- **Predicts**: Approve / Deny
- **Identifies**: Risk factors
- **Recommends**: Corrections before submission
- **Impact**: 40-50% reduction in denials

## Model Performance

- **Accuracy**: 64%+
- **Precision**: 68% (Approved), 57% (Denied)
- **Top Factors**: Prior auth, documentation, coding

## Business Impact

At $50M in annual claims:
- 12% denial rate = $6M denied
- Reduce to 6% = $3M recovered
- ROI: 10-20x implementation cost
